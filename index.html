📧 Email Automation
Automated emails are sent at 10–11 PM daily (calling report) and 15 minutes after each Sabha ends Attendance Reprot.
⚙️ Automation Settings
📞 Daily Report → Admin & Moderator
Sends nightly volunteer calling stats with PDF attachment to all admins and moderators at 10–11 PM
OFF
📞 Daily Report → Active Volunteers
Sends each volunteer their personal calling stats at 10–11 PM (no contact list, only counts)
OFF
🙏 Sabha Attendance → Volunteers
Sends personalized only assigned contacts attendance to email (no PDF) to each volunteer automatically 15 min after Sabha ends
OFF
🛡️ Sabha Report → Admins & Moderators
Sends total present/absent stats with full attendance PDF to admins and moderators automatically 15 min after Sabha ends
OFF

🎂 Birthday Calendar Sync
📅 Google Calendar Status

Calendar

Auto Sync
Last sync: checking…
Contacts with a DOB are synced as yearly recurring all-day birthday events in Google Calendar.
Each event includes contact profile, Sabha attendance, and a WhatsApp greeting link.
⚡ Actions
🔍 Sync Single Contact by ID
Enter a SevakCall Contact ID to sync just that contact's birthday to Google Calendar.
📤 Bulk Sync to Google Calendar
Syncs all contacts (with DOB) to the "SevakCall Birthdays" calendar. Creates new events or updates existing ones. Safe to run multiple times.
⏰ Automatic Daily Sync
Run setupBirthdayTrigger() once from your Apps Script editor.
After that, the calendar auto-syncs every night at 1 AM — new contacts and edits are picked up automatically.
🗑️ Delete All Birthday Events
Permanently removes all SevakCall birthday events from Google Calendar and clears the sync tracking sheet. Use only for a full reset.
🎂 Birthday Daily Summary Email
Every morning at 6 AM IST a birthday summary is automatically emailed to all active Admins and Moderators with a pre-filled WhatsApp greeting button for each contact.
